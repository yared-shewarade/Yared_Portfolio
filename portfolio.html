Stock Market Project

This project studies the impact of news on the stock market. It uses API to find the news and it leverages VaderSentiment package to quantify the news. Then we plot Stock Prices and Stock Volumnes against Vader Scores. The news source is from: New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and CNN. The stock market data is from: Alpha Vantage.

Where to Work Project

This project studies about where and which compnaies is the best to work as Data Scientist in the United States. It identifies the most seek-after skills from more than 4000 job descriptions and it explores the correlations among location, company, salary and job rating.
